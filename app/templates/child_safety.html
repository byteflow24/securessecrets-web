Child Safety Standards

Our commitment to protecting children and preventing exploitation

Our Policy

SecuresSecrets strictly prohibits any form of child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or exploitation of minors. We are committed to maintaining a safe platform and take violations of these standards very seriously.

Enforcement

  • No CSAM Allowed: Any attempt to create, store, or share child sexual abuse material is forbidden and will result in immediate action.
  • Law Enforcement: We comply with regional and national authorities and report any suspected CSAM as required by law.
  • User Reports: Users can report child safety concerns directly through the app or by email.

Our Commitment

We are committed to protecting children, supporting safe use of our app, and working with global child protection agencies when necessary. All reports are handled promptly and confidentially.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Child Safety Standards or wish to report a concern, please contact us at support@securessecrets.com .

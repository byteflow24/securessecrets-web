How We Keep Your Secrets Safe

End-to-End Encryption

Secrets are encrypted on creation using strong algorithms, ensuring only the intended recipient can read them.

Secure Cloud Storage

Secrets are stored encrypted at rest in a secure cloud environment with industry-standard protections.

Timed Auto-Reveal

Secrets can be automatically revealed at a set time or after a period of inactivity, minimizing manual risk.

Private Sharing Options

You choose whether secrets are shared publicly or privately through secure email — you're in control.

Auto-Destruction

Secrets can be deleted automatically after reveal or after a set time, ensuring your data doesn’t linger.

Zero Access

Even we can’t read your secrets. Only users with the correct links can ever decrypt and view them.

Access Control

Secrets are linked to your account, giving you full control over who sees what — and when.

Verified Delivery

We track reveal status and give you visibility into when and how your secrets are accessed or shared.
